Alson Wong is Owl Vlogs, a college student who wants to go hiking but can't because of the ongoing pandemic. A former 5'9" outside hitter, opposite hitter, and jump server for the Stamford Lions Mens Varsity Volleyball team in high school, Alson was part of a squad that were the champions of the ACSIS league and placed second at the SEASAC tournament. Since he's graduated, the lockdown has kept him from playing volleyball anywere. Thus, he's looking for a team to play in. He is currently studying for an engineering degree in university.

Besides volleyball, Alson is into aviation, videogames (though that might have changed after getting tilted on VALORANT), and hiking. The latter is pretty much why the Owl Vlogs channel exists. Many of his school trips took him to places with his friends that he would never have gone alone, allowing him to connect with nature, tranquility, and the beauty of having mountains around you and friends to keep you company. Once restrictions lift, Alson plans to gather as many friends as he can to hike the world together. Oh yeah, he likes filming his trips too. Hence the channel.
